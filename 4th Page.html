Muni "Lily" He

Lily Muni He was born in Chengdu, Sichuan, China. Her fatherwas a hotelier and restaurateur who introduced her to golf at a young age and by age 5, she was already playing competitively. Her family moved to Vancouver and later, San Diego.

She studied at Torrey Pines High School in San Diego. In the United States, she was able to play golf more regularly and she joined San Diego and Southern California junior golf tournaments, alongside U.S. Kids' Golf and American Junior Golf Association events.Before turning pro, she spent one year at the University of Southern California, majoring in communications.
